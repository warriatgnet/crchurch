IN THE BEGINNING
Soon after completing his secondary school in premier sec, school Ukehe in 1989. Nwodo Charles followed his uncle ENGR. Alfred Ozoemena (alias) KIZITO LEE LTD to Abuja; to serve him as an apprentice. After 5years he heard the voice of God one faithful morning. The voice said, “My son go to Lagos State, I will use 20years to train you as my prophet. After series of prayer, fasting and meditation on the word of God. I moved to Lagos with an address. By the grace of God; he completed his training as God said. He left Abuja on 20 December, 1995 and came to Lagos on 12th January, 1996.
